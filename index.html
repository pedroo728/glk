Tips

Bathroom Remodeling Tips

Streamline the Vanity

Double sinks have long been popular, but having just one gives you more counter space, saves the expense of an extra sink and faucet, and provides more cabinet storage.

Conceal the Toilet

Setting the toilet apart with a partial wall or door provides privacy and makes for a more attractive bathroom.

Layer the Light

Overhead fixtures can cast shadows on your face. Instead, create layers of light with wall-mounted sconces or vertical fixtures on either side of the vanity.
